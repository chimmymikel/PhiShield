About PhiShield

Our Mission

PhiShield is a comprehensive phishing detection system designed to protect users from malicious links and suspicious messages in today's digital landscape.

What We Do
  • Analyze URLs for phishing indicators and suspicious patterns
  • Scan text messages and emails for phishing content
  • Provide real-time threat assessment and risk levels
  • Educate users about phishing prevention best practices
  • Maintain history of security checks for user reference
Key Features
URL Analysis

Deep analysis of links for suspicious domains, keywords, and patterns

Message Scanning

Content analysis for phishing attempts in text and email messages

Check History

Comprehensive logging of all security checks and results

Risk Assessment

Three-tier risk classification (Safe, Suspicious, Dangerous)
